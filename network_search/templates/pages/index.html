    • Welcome to Compass. This tool is designed to help Communities In Schools staff find and connect with affiliates and state offices across the network; engage with nationally vetted partnerships; and identify evidence-based programs being delivered with fidelity at the local level.

    How does it work?

    • Affiliates: Search by affiliate size, model implementation, student unique needs, budget, or other key indicators.
    • Programs: Search by demographics and program details, including tiers of evidence.
    • Partners: Search by grade, core service category, tiers of service and organizational reach.

    To begin a search, click on any of the three labeled tabs: Affiliates, Programs, or Partners.
